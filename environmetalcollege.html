Today Butwal College

Environmental is a Neapl tertiary education institute with campuses in Butwal, Bhairawa, and other towns.
It was formed in May 2016 after the amalgamation of Rupandehi and Butwal Institute of Technology. Butwal Institue is the second largest university in Nepal, with 100,000 alumni around the world. We're located in Butwal, the third best city in the country to live in.
